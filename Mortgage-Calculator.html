Frequently Asked Questions

Mortgage Calculator - FAQs

What inputs do I need to use the mortgage calculator?

You typically need the loan amount (principal), the annual interest rate, and the loan term (years). Some calculators also allow you to add down payment, property tax, insurance, or extra monthly payments for a more detailed estimate.

Does the calculator include taxes and insurance in the monthly payment?

Most basic mortgage calculators do not include property taxes or homeowners insurance by default â€” they estimate only principal and interest. If you want a full monthly cost, use a calculator that lets you add estimated taxes, insurance, and HOA fees.

Can I use the mortgage calculator for adjustable-rate mortgages (ARMs)?

Basic calculators assume a fixed interest rate. For ARMs, use a calculator that supports rate changes over time or run scenarios with different rates to estimate how payments may change when the rate adjusts.
