How BMI Calculator Works

This calculator measures your Body Mass Index (BMI) based on your height and weight. BMI is a screening tool that categorizes individuals into weight categories that may indicate health risks.

Instructions

  1. Select your preferred measurement system (Metric or Imperial)
  2. Enter your height and weight
  3. Click "Calculate BMI" button
  4. View your BMI result and category
  5. Compare with the standard BMI classification table

Advantages

  • Quick health screening tool
  • Simple to use and understand
  • Helps identify potential weight problems
  • Free and accessible anytime

Limitations

  • Doesn't account for muscle mass
  • Doesn't consider body composition
  • May not be accurate for athletes
  • Doesn't differentiate between fat and muscle

Frequently Asked Questions

What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a numerical value calculated from your weight and height. It provides a simple way to categorize individuals as underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

Is BMI accurate for everyone?

BMI is a useful screening tool but has limitations. It may not accurately reflect body fat percentage in athletes, pregnant women, or the elderly. Always consult a healthcare professional for a comprehensive health assessment.

What should I do if my BMI is high?

If your BMI indicates overweight or obesity, consider consulting a doctor or nutritionist. They can help develop a personalized plan including diet, exercise, and lifestyle changes.
